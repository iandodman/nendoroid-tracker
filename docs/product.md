# Product Vision

## What is Nendodex?

Nendodex is a web application built exclusively for Nendoroid collectors. It provides an official catalog of Nendoroids and allows users to manage their personal collection in a simple, modern and enjoyable way.

The goal is not only to store information, but to make collecting easier and more engaging through a user experience designed specifically for Nendoroid enthusiasts.

## Why does it exist?

Many collectors want to keep track of their collection, wishlist and purchase history, but the available tools are often complex, outdated or not focused exclusively on Nendoroids.

Nendodex exists to provide a dedicated solution that is intuitive, fast and pleasant to use, allowing collectors to focus on enjoying their hobby instead of managing spreadsheets or navigating complicated interfaces.

## Target Audience

Nendodex is designed for people who collect Nendoroids, regardless of whether they own a handful of figures or hundreds.

The application is built around the needs of this community rather than serving as a generic figure database.

## Product Principles

The project is guided by the following principles:

* Simplicity over complexity.
* Mobile-first, desktop-friendly experience.
* Search first.
* Collection first.
* Fast and intuitive navigation.
* Designed for collectors, not databases.

Whenever a new feature is considered, it should answer two questions:

1. Does it improve the collector's experience?
2. Does it keep the application simple to use?

If the answer to both questions is yes, it is likely a good addition to the product.

## Minimum Viable Product (MVP)

The first public version of Nendodex should include:

* Google authentication.
* Personal collection.
* Wishlist.
* Official Nendoroid catalog.
* Global search by number, name or series.
* Catalog browsing.
* Nendoroid detail page.
* Basic user profile.
* Mobile-first responsive design.

## User Experience Goals

The application should feel fast and effortless.

The search bar is the primary action of the application and should always be easily accessible.

The catalog should prioritize visual browsing through compact cards displaying:

* Image
* Nendoroid number
* Name
* Series

Users should immediately know whether a Nendoroid:

* belongs to their collection;
* belongs to their wishlist.

The detail page should focus on the figure itself while keeping collection actions easily reachable on mobile devices.

## Long-Term Vision

The ambition of Nendodex is to become the first application that Nendoroid collectors think of when they want to manage or explore their collection.

Future versions may include:

* News.
* Upcoming releases.
* Public profiles.
* Collection sharing.
* Statistics.
* Community features.
* Notifications.
